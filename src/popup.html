Comment Wizard

Hover over each option for additional information.
Show incoming comments* Adds a new tab for incoming comments in the comment section. The outgoing comment list stays the original list from webtoon aside from the additional tab bar in the top.
Hide delete button* Hides the delete button from all tiles in the dashboard to avoid accidentally deleting your series.
Reorder buttons in header Changes the order of the buttons in the top right corner of the header.
Hide rating Hides the rating field from the comic overview page. You can not rate anyone's comic but others can still rate yours!
Round sub counter to thousand Removes last three digits once sub counter is > 1000.

* applies only when you are logged in

Refresh the page after changing settings. Some settings might take up to 10 minutes to take effect.
